## What is a Domain?

Your domain is **your brand's global address on the internet**. It's the unique name that people use to find and access your website, serving as your digital identity worldwide.

<Info>
A domain is like the physical address of your business, but for the internet. Just as customers need your street address to visit your store, they need your domain to visit your website.
</Info>

## Domain Best Practices

### Choosing the Right Domain

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep It Short and Simple">
    Shorter domains are easier to remember, type, and share. Aim for 2-3 words maximum.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Make It Memorable">
    Choose a name that's distinctive and easy to recall. Avoid complex spellings or numbers.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Use Keywords">
    Include relevant keywords that describe your business or service for better recognition.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Consider Multiple Extensions">
    Register your domain with multiple extensions (.com, .es, etc.) to protect your brand.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Avoid Hyphens and Numbers">
    These make domains harder to communicate verbally and can appear less professional.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical DNS Management

We handle all technical domain configuration:

### DNS Records

- **A Records**: Point your domain to your web server
- **MX Records**: Configure email delivery
- **CNAME Records**: Set up subdomains (www, blog, shop, etc.)
- **TXT Records**: Verify domain ownership and email authentication
- **NS Records**: Manage nameserver configuration

### Cloudflare Integration

All domains are configured with Cloudflare for:

- Fast DNS resolution (globally distributed)
- DDoS protection at the DNS level
- SSL certificate provisioning
- Advanced DNS security features
- Real-time DNS analytics

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Who owns the domain?">
    When we register a domain for you, it is registered **in your name**. You are the legal owner, and we manage it on your behalf. You can transfer it away at any time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if my domain expires?">
    We monitor all domain expirations and send reminders well in advance. If a domain expires, there's typically a 30-day grace period for renewal, followed by a redemption period where recovery is still possible (with additional fees).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I use a domain I already own?">
    Absolutely! We can either transfer your domain to our management or simply configure it to point to your hosting without transferring ownership.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How long does domain registration take?">
    Most domain registrations are instant. However, some extensions (especially country-specific ones) may require additional verification and take 24-48 hours.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I change my domain later?">
    Yes, you can register a new domain and redirect or migrate your website. However, we recommend choosing carefully as your domain becomes part of your brand identity.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
