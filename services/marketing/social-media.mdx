## Content Calendar

### Organized, Consistent Publishing

We develop and maintain a **content calendar** to ensure:

- **Consistency**: Regular posting schedule maintains engagement
- **Variety**: Mix of content types keeps feed interesting
- **Planning**: Strategic content aligned with business goals
- **Seasonality**: Timely content for holidays and events
- **Flexibility**: Ability to adapt to trends and news

<Note>
**Typical Posting Frequency:**
- Instagram: 3-7 posts per week + daily Stories
- Facebook: 3-5 posts per week
- Twitter: 1-3 tweets per day
- LinkedIn: 2-5 posts per week
- TikTok: 3-7 videos per week
</Note>
