## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines That Get Opened

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Subject Line Tips">
    - **Keep it Short**: 40-50 characters for mobile
    - **Create Curiosity**: Make people want to open
    - **Use Numbers**: "5 Ways to..." performs well
    - **Personalization**: Include recipient's name or details
    - **Avoid Spam Words**: "FREE!", "ACT NOW!", excessive punctuation
    - **Test Everything**: A/B test to find what works for your audience
    
    **Good Examples:**
    - "[Name], your exclusive invitation inside"
    - "Quick question about your website"
    - "5 mistakes that are costing you customers"
    - "Last chance: Sale ends tonight"
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Content Best Practices">
    - **Mobile-First**: 60%+ of emails are opened on mobile
    - **Scannable**: Use short paragraphs, bullets, and headings
    - **Single Focus**: One main message and CTA per email
    - **Visual Appeal**: Balance text with images
    - **Personalization**: Use subscriber data to customize content
    - **Clear CTA**: Make the desired action obvious
    - **Value First**: Give before you ask
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Timing & Frequency">
    - **Optimal Send Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM-2 PM (test for your audience)
    - **Consistency**: Stick to a regular schedule
    - **Not Too Often**: Avoid email fatigue (1-4 times per month for most businesses)
    - **Segment Sending**: Different frequencies for different subscriber segments
    - **Respect Preferences**: Let subscribers choose frequency
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Email Analytics

### Measuring Success

We track and optimize based on key metrics:

| Metric | What It Measures | Good Benchmark |
|--------|------------------|----------------|
| **Open Rate** | % who open the email | 15-25% |
| **Click Rate** | % who click a link | 2-5% |
| **Click-to-Open Rate** | % of openers who click | 10-15% |
| **Conversion Rate** | % who complete desired action | 1-5% |
| **Bounce Rate** | % of undelivered emails | &lt;2% |
| **Unsubscribe Rate** | % who opt out | &lt;0.5% |
| **List Growth Rate** | Net new subscribers | 2-5% monthly |

<Note>
Benchmarks vary by industry. We compare your performance to industry standards and focus on continuous improvement.
</Note>

## Deliverability Optimization

### Ensuring Your Emails Reach Inboxes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Technical Setup">
    - Configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records
    - Use authenticated sending domain
    - Maintain good IP reputation
    - Monitor blacklist status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="List Hygiene">
    - Remove hard bounces immediately
    - Re-engage or remove inactive subscribers
    - Use double opt-in for new subscribers
    - Honor unsubscribe requests instantly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Content Quality">
    - Avoid spam trigger words
    - Balance text and images
    - Include plain text version
    - Always include unsubscribe link
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Engagement">
    - Send to engaged subscribers more often
    - Segment and target appropriately
    - Maintain consistent sending patterns
    - Encourage replies and interaction
  </Step>
</Steps>
