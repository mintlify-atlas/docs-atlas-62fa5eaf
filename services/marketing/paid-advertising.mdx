## Paid Advertising (SEM)

**Paid advertising from Google Ads (SEM) or Facebook Ads allows you to reach customers faster than organic positioning.** While SEO takes months to show results, paid ads can drive traffic and sales starting day one.

<Info>
**SEM** (Search Engine Marketing) typically refers to paid search ads on Google, while paid social advertising includes platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
</Info>

## Google Ads (SEM)

### Search Advertising

**Appear at the top of Google search results** when potential customers search for your products or services.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Search Ads">
    **Text ads on Google search results:**
    
    - **High Intent**: Reach people actively searching for solutions
    - **Keyword Targeting**: Bid on relevant search terms
    - **Ad Extensions**: Additional information (phone, location, links)
    - **Quality Score**: Lower costs through relevance and quality
    - **SERP Dominance**: Appear above organic results
    
    **Best For**: Lead generation, high-intent purchases, service businesses
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Shopping Ads">
    **Product listings with images and prices:**
    
    - **Visual Product Showcase**: Show products directly in search
    - **Price Transparency**: Display prices upfront
    - **Product Feed**: Automated from your e-commerce catalog
    - **Comparison Shopping**: Compete directly with other sellers
    - **Mobile Optimization**: Prominent placement on mobile
    
    **Best For**: E-commerce, product sales, price-competitive markets
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Display Ads">
    **Banner ads across Google's Display Network:**
    
    - **2 Million+ Websites**: Massive reach across the web
    - **Visual Advertising**: Images, GIFs, HTML5 animations
    - **Remarketing**: Target previous website visitors
    - **Awareness Building**: Build brand recognition
    - **Audience Targeting**: Demographics, interests, behaviors
    
    **Best For**: Brand awareness, remarketing, visual products
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Video Ads (YouTube)">
    **Video ads on YouTube and partner sites:**
    
    - **Massive Audience**: 2+ billion YouTube users
    - **Engaging Format**: Video storytelling
    - **Skippable & Non-Skippable**: Different ad formats
    - **Targeting Options**: Interests, keywords, placements
    - **Cost-Effective**: Pay per view or engagement
    
    **Best For**: Brand building, product demonstrations, storytelling
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Local Services Ads">
    **Verified local service provider listings:**
    
    - **Google Screened Badge**: Trust and credibility
    - **Pay Per Lead**: Only pay for qualified leads
    - **Top Placement**: Appear above all other ads
    - **Phone Call Integration**: Direct customer calls
    - **Service Area Targeting**: Geographic precision
    
    **Best For**: Local services (plumbers, lawyers, cleaners, etc.)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Facebook & Instagram Ads

### Social Media Advertising

**Reach your audience where they spend their time** with highly targeted social media advertising.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Facebook Ads">
    **Powerful targeting on the world's largest social network:**
    
    **Ad Formats:**
    - Single image or video
    - Carousel (multiple images)
    - Collection ads
    - Instant Experience (full-screen mobile)
    - Lead generation forms
    - Messenger ads
    
    **Targeting Options:**
    - Demographics (age, gender, location, language)
    - Interests and hobbies
    - Behaviors and purchase intent

    - Connections (fans, friends of fans)
    - Custom Audiences (email lists, website visitors)
    - Lookalike Audiences (similar to your customers)

  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Instagram Ads">
    **Visual advertising on the world's most engaged platform:**
    
    **Ad Formats:**
    - Feed posts (image or video)
    - Stories ads (full-screen vertical)
    - Reels ads (short-form video)
    - Explore ads
    - Shopping ads (product tags)
    - Carousel ads
    
    **Targeting:**
    - Same powerful targeting as Facebook
    - Platform automatically optimized for Instagram audience
    - Can run simultaneously on Facebook and Instagram
    
    **Best For**: Visual products, fashion, food, lifestyle, younger audiences
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Audience Network">
    **Extend reach beyond Facebook and Instagram:**
    
    - Third-party apps and websites
    - Native ad placements
    - Consistent targeting across platforms
    - Additional reach and frequency
    - Often lower costs than Facebook/Instagram placements
    
    **Best For**: Maximizing reach and frequency
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Budget Recommendations

### How Much Should You Invest?

<Warning>
**Minimum Budgets:**

While you can start with any budget, we recommend minimum daily budgets for effective campaigns:

- **Google Search Ads**: €20-50/day minimum
- **Google Shopping**: €30-75/day minimum
- **Facebook/Instagram**: €15-30/day minimum

Lower budgets limit testing ability and may not be sufficient for competitive markets.

<Steps>

## Remarketing & Retargeting

### Re-engage Previous Visitors

<Info>
**Remarketing** shows ads to people who previously visited your website but didn't convert. These "warm" audiences convert at 2-3x higher rates than cold traffic.
</Info>

**Remarketing Strategies:**

- **Site Visitors**: Target anyone who visited your site
- **Cart Abandoners**: Special offers for those who left items in cart
- **Product Viewers**: Show ads for specific products they viewed
- **Past Customers**: Cross-sell and upsell to previous buyers
- **Video Viewers**: Target people who watched your videos
- **Engaged Users**: Focus on those who spent time on your site

## SEM vs. SEO: The Comparison

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Speed">
    **SEM**: Immediate results, traffic starts day one
    
    **SEO**: Slow build, 3-12 months for results
    
    **Winner**: SEM for immediate needs, SEO for long-term
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Cost">
    **SEM**: Pay for every click, ongoing expense
    
    **SEO**: Upfront investment, "free" long-term traffic
    
    **Winner**: SEO for long-term ROI, SEM for controlled spend
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Control">
    **SEM**: Complete control over budget, targeting, messaging
    
    **SEO**: Limited control, dependent on Google's algorithm
    
    **Winner**: SEM for predictability and control
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Sustainability">
    **SEM**: Traffic stops when budget ends
    
    **SEO**: Sustainable traffic without ongoing costs
    
    **Winner**: SEO for long-term sustainability
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Tip>
**Best Approach**: Use SEM for immediate results and testing, while building long-term SEO. As SEO traffic grows, reduce SEM dependency and costs.
</Tip>
