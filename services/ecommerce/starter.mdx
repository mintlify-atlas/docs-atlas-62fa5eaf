## Technical Details

### Payment Methods
All major payment methods are integrated and ready to use:
- PayPal Business Account
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Additional payment gateways available

### Product Management
Easy-to-use admin panel that allows you to:
- Add and edit products
- Manage inventory levels
- Set prices and discounts
- Upload product images
- Create product variations (sizes, colors, etc.)

### Shipping Configuration
Two shipping methods can be configured for:
- Different geographic zones
- Weight-based pricing
- Flat rate or free shipping options
- Shipping calculations at checkout

## Limitations

<Warning>
  Be aware of these limitations with Tienda Start:
</Warning>

- Hosting included for only 6 months (renewal required)
- Limited to 100 products
- Only 1 language supported
- Basic backup schedule (monthly)
- Technical support for 1 month only
- No blog functionality

Consider upgrading to [Tienda PRO](/services/ecommerce/pro) if you need:

- Weekly backups
- Blog functionality
- More email accounts and contact forms
