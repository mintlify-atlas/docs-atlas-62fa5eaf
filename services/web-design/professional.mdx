## When to Choose Professional Over Premium

Choose **Professional** if:
- 10 pages are sufficient for your content
- You need up to 3 languages (not 5)
- Weekly backups meet your needs
- You want the best value for money

Choose **[Premium](/services/web-design/premium)** if:
- You need 11-15 pages
- You require 4-5 languages
- You need daily backups
- You want Cloudflare security
- You need technical service (more comprehensive than maintenance)
