## OnePage Structure Example

A typical OnePage website includes these 5 sections:

1. **Hero Section** - Eye-catching introduction with your main value proposition
2. **About** - Who you are and what makes you unique
3. **Services/Products** - What you offer to your customers
4. **Testimonials/Portfolio** - Social proof and examples of your work
5. **Contact** - How to reach you, including the contact form
